## Decision 040: The Project Uses Separate Specialized Documents

**Status:** Locked for Current Workflow
**Category:** Project organization

### Decision

The novel is developed through separate files for:

- Narrative Brief
- Story Bible
- Character Bible
- World and Technology Rules
- Master Timeline
- Plot Outline and Chapter Map
- Chapter Blueprint Template
- Individual Chapter Blueprints
- Style Guide
- Continuity Ledger
- Research and Plausibility Ledger
- Decision Log
- Manuscript chapters

### Reason

No single file should carry every kind of information.

Specialized documents make handoffs, revisions, and continuity checks easier.

### Consequences

Every document must stay within its purpose.

Changes must propagate to affected files.

### Affected Documents

- Novel Development and Canon Guide
- Entire project structure

### Reconsider Only If

The workflow becomes too fragmented and a controlled consolidation clearly improves usability.
