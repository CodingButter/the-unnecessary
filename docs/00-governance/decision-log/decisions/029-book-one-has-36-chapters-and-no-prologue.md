## Decision 029: Book One Has 36 Chapters and No Prologue

**Status:** Active but Revisable
**Category:** Structure

### Decision

The current outline contains 36 chapters.

The novel begins directly with Eli discovering that his phone has lost service.

There is no prologue.

### Reason

The opening should ground the reader in ordinary life rather than begin with history, Mars, or a dramatic future event.

The reader learns the world through immediate consequences.

### Consequences

- Chapter One is “No Signal.”
- Kade and Mars first appear later.
- Historical exposition must emerge through story.
- The opening and ending mirror one another through communication loss and restoration.

### Affected Documents

- Plot Outline
- Master Timeline
- Chapter Blueprint files

### Reconsider Only If

Detailed blueprinting reveals a clear structural need to merge or split chapters. A prologue should only be added if it accomplishes something the direct opening cannot.
