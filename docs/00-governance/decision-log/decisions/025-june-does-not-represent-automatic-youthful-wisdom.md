## Decision 025: June Does Not Represent Automatic Youthful Wisdom

**Status:** Locked for Current Draft
**Category:** Character

### Decision

June is gifted, bold, and more comfortable with abandoned technology than older characters.

She is also reckless, incomplete in her knowledge, and capable of repeating the consent failures she criticizes.

### Reason

She should not become a magical young genius whose confidence always proves correct.

### Consequences

- Her unauthorized expansions create real risk.
- Morrow can use her trust.
- Her mistakes must have consequences.
- She learns that decentralization does not guarantee freedom.

### Affected Documents

- Character Bible
- Plot Outline
- Chapter Blueprints

### Reconsider Only If

Her skill set changes. Her limitations should remain meaningful.
