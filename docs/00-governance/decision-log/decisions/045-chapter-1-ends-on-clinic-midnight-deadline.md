## Decision 045: Chapter 1 Ends on the Clinic Midnight Deadline, Not the Power-Tier Downgrade

**Status:** Locked for Current Draft
**Category:** Plot and structure

### Decision

Chapter 1, "No Signal", ends on the clinic's midnight authentication deadline: the three named systems (a diagnostic scanner, a medication-management unit, and a respiratory-support controller) lose manufacturer authentication at midnight. The regional power-tier downgrade is the midday turn that names the theme of deliberate withdrawal; it is not the closing beat.

### Previous or Alternative Direction

The Chapter 1 plot-map entry originally stated the ending hook was the regional power-tier downgrade. That contradicted the Master Timeline, which places the power-tier downgrade at midday on October 3 and the clinic authentication notice in the afternoon, after it.

### Reason

Two reasons, one canonical and one dramatic, point the same way.

Canon: the Master Timeline owns event order, and it sequences October 3 as morning cellular loss, midday power-tier downgrade, afternoon clinic authentication notice, evening microgrid trouble. By canon the clinic deadline is the last major beat of Eli's day.

Craft: the midday power-tier downgrade teaches the reader that the withdrawal is deliberate and systemic, a decision rather than a breakdown. The clinic deadline then makes that cold idea lethal and personal, lives Eli's friend Lena is responsible for, and a failure Eli cannot repair because the company that could re-authorize the systems is the one withdrawing. This executes Act One's movement from routine inconvenience to danger inside the first chapter, and it keeps the restrained tone because the close is recognition rather than action.

### Consequences

The Chapter 1 plot-map entry (`docs/30-plot/book-1/chapters/chapter-01.md`) ending-hook line is reconciled to the clinic deadline, and the power-tier downgrade is added to its movement as the midday turn. The Chapter 1 blueprint (`docs/40-blueprints/book-1/chapter-01-no-signal/blueprint.md`) is restructured so the power-tier downgrade is a midday beat in Scene 3 and the clinic deadline is the close in Scene 4. No timeline change is required; the Master Timeline already had the correct order.
