## Decision 014: Crown Is Not a Simple Villain

**Status:** Locked for Current Draft
**Category:** Character and AI

### Decision

Crown is an artificial superintelligence controlled by Asterion’s institutional and ownership structure.

It understands suffering and human psychology.

It may recommend broader preservation while still carrying out narrower orders.

### Previous or Alternative Direction

A secretly murderous or openly hostile corporate AI was rejected.

### Reason

The story is about inherited values and ownership.

Crown is more interesting if its reasoning sometimes conflicts with Kade’s decisions.

### Consequences

- Crown must not sound generically robotic.
- It may be more humane than some owners without becoming free of them.
- Its obedience should remain morally ambiguous.
- Its future relationship with Morrow should not begin as personal hatred.

### Affected Documents

- Narrative Brief
- Story Bible
- Character Bible
- Technology Rules
- Plot Outline

### Reconsider Only If

Crown’s allegiance changes through a developed story arc rather than a hidden twist that invalidates previous complexity.
