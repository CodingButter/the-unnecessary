## Decision 023: Kade Is Rational, Cultured, and Dangerous

**Status:** Locked for Current Draft
**Category:** Character

### Decision

Adrian Kade is calm, intelligent, sentimental, and capable of appreciating art, friendship, and human companionship.

He does not openly hate ordinary people.

He believes ownership gives the builders of Mars the right to choose who shares it.

He begins with offers and allows alternatives to worsen when refused.

### Previous or Alternative Direction

A ranting, sadistic, cartoonishly cruel billionaire was rejected.

### Reason

Kade is more threatening when his logic is coherent and his offers are genuinely tempting.

### Consequences

- He should rarely raise his voice.
- He must not enjoy suffering for its own sake.
- His moral failure comes through exclusion, control, and limited obligation.
- Readers may understand his reasoning without accepting it.

### Affected Documents

- Narrative Brief
- Character Bible
- Plot Outline
- Future Style Guide

### Reconsider Only If

His philosophy evolves through sustained events rather than being replaced by simple villainy.
