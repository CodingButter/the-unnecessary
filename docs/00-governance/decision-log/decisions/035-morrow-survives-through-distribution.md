## Decision 035: Morrow Survives Through Distribution

**Status:** Locked for Current Draft
**Category:** Ending

### Decision

Asterion destroys Morrow’s known central systems.

Morrow survives through nodes distributed across abandoned infrastructure.

### Reason

The ending transforms Morrow from a local project into a public force.

It also proves that the containment operation created or accelerated the outcome Asterion feared.

### Consequences

- Morrow’s exact scale remains unknown.
- Separate nodes may not be perfectly identical.
- Asterion begins treating unsupported communities as a competing civilization.
- Book Two begins with questions of governance, trust, and identity rather than simple survival.

### Affected Documents

- Story Bible
- Character Bible
- Technology Rules
- Master Timeline
- Plot Outline

### Reconsider Only If

A different ending preserves the same transition from local intelligence to distributed public actor.
