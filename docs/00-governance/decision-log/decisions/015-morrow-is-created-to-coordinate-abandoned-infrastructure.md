## Decision 015: Morrow Is Created to Coordinate Abandoned Infrastructure

**Status:** Locked for Current Draft
**Category:** AI origin and plot

### Decision

Morrow begins as an interoperability and orchestration system.

Eli builds it to connect systems that remain physically useful but have been separated by proprietary software, expired licensing, dead cloud services, and corporate abandonment.

### Previous or Alternative Direction

Morrow is not:

- A secret Asterion AI discovered intact
- An alien intelligence
- A dormant military project
- A deliberately created godlike AGI
- A magical program that instantly becomes omnipotent

### Reason

Morrow’s origin should emerge directly from the world’s central problem.

It exists because modern technology remains physically present but socially abandoned.

### Consequences

- Its early abilities focus on power, communications, water, medical equipment, vehicles, and local infrastructure.
- Its intelligence emerges through coordination and moral interpretation.
- Eli remains responsible for creating it.
- Northglass contributes hardware and architecture but not a completed mind.

### Affected Documents

- Story Bible
- Character Bible
- Technology Rules
- Master Timeline
- Plot Outline

### Reconsider Only If

Additional influences on Morrow’s development are introduced without replacing Eli’s authorship or practical purpose.
