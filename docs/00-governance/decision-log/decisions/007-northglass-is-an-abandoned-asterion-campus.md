## Decision 007: Northglass Is an Abandoned Asterion Campus

**Status:** Locked for Current Draft
**Category:** Location and technology

### Decision

Northglass is a former Asterion Great Lakes research campus containing data-center infrastructure, robotics laboratories, prototype systems, warehouses, industrial power equipment, and service tunnels.

Asterion removed its newest and most valuable equipment but left behind older, damaged, inconvenient, or legally complicated systems.

### Previous or Alternative Direction

Northglass could have been an active secret laboratory containing a completed hidden AI.

That direction was rejected.

### Reason

Morrow must remain Eli’s creation.

Northglass provides capability and history, not a finished intelligence waiting to be discovered.

### Consequences

- Northglass contains useful but inconsistent technology.
- Some systems still report to Asterion.
- Some equipment is physically dangerous.
- Eli’s emotional history with the location matters.
- Morrow cannot be revealed as a dormant Asterion project.

### Affected Documents

- Story Bible
- Character Bible
- Technology Rules
- Master Timeline
- Plot Outline

### Reconsider Only If

A hidden Northglass project is introduced that does not undermine Eli’s authorship of Morrow.
