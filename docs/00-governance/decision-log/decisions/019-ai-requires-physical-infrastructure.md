## Decision 019: AI Requires Physical Infrastructure

**Status:** Locked for Current Draft
**Category:** Technology

### Decision

All artificial intelligence requires:

- Hardware
- electricity
- cooling
- storage
- networks
- sensors
- maintenance
- physical access
- manufacturing
- human or robotic support

### Reason

The story must not use AI as magic.

Physical limits create meaningful stakes and prevent convenient solutions.

### Consequences

- Hidden computing generates heat.
- Damaged hardware can remove memory or capability.
- Network isolation matters.
- Advanced processors cannot be fabricated in a neighborhood workshop.
- Robots require parts and maintenance.
- Morrow and Crown can be powerful without being omnipotent.

### Affected Documents

- Technology Rules
- Plot Outline
- Chapter Blueprints
- Research Ledger

### Reconsider Only If

Specific technological advances are supported by research and added deliberately to the Technology Rules.
