## Decision 038: Avoid Exaggerated Cyberpunk Aesthetics

**Status:** Locked for Current Draft
**Category:** Style and setting

### Decision

The story may contain themes associated with cyberpunk, including corporate power, technological inequality, surveillance, and ownership.

It should avoid relying on constant neon, theatrical body modification, visual clutter, giant holograms, or stylized street gangs.

### Reason

The project’s visual identity depends on the unsettling familiarity of ordinary places.

### Consequences

- Technology should often be hidden inside familiar objects and infrastructure.
- Advanced systems can exist in normal buildings.
- Protected enclaves should look maintained rather than fantastical.
- “Cyberpunk” should not dominate marketing language until the final prose establishes whether it fits.

### Affected Documents

- Narrative Brief
- Story Bible
- Future Style Guide
- Chapter Blueprints

### Reconsider Only If

A specific environment naturally uses one of these aesthetics without redefining the whole setting.
