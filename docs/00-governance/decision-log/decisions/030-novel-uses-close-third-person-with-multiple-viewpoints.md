## Decision 030: The Novel Uses Close Third Person With Multiple Viewpoints

**Status:** Locked for Current Draft
**Category:** Narrative structure

### Decision

Eli is the primary viewpoint.

Secondary viewpoints include Jonah, Kade, Lena, June, Mara, and Sera.

Morrow and Crown do not receive direct viewpoints in Book One.

### Reason

Multiple viewpoints reveal areas Eli cannot access while preserving uncertainty around the artificial intelligences.

### Consequences

- Viewpoint changes must have a clear purpose.
- No scene should reveal information the viewpoint character cannot know.
- Eli receives approximately half the chapters.
- Supporting characters without chapters can still remain important.

### Affected Documents

- Narrative Brief
- Character Bible
- Plot Outline
- Chapter Blueprints
- Future Style Guide

### Reconsider Only If

A viewpoint proves unnecessary or another character gains a clearly distinct narrative function.
