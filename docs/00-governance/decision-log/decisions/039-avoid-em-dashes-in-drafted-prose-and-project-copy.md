## Decision 039: Avoid Em Dashes in Drafted Prose and Project Copy

**Status:** Active but Revisable
**Category:** Style preference

### Decision

Use commas, periods, colons, parentheses, or sentence restructuring instead of em dashes in drafted prose and project documents.

### Reason

Em dashes can make generated writing feel recognizably AI-produced when overused.

### Consequences

The future Style Guide should formalize this preference.

### Affected Documents

- Style Guide
- Manuscript
- Chapter Blueprints
- Marketing copy

### Reconsider Only If

A deliberate house style later permits rare, controlled use.
