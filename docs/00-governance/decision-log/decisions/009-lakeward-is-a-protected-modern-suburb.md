## Decision 009: Lakeward Is a Protected Modern Suburb

**Status:** Active but Revisable
**Category:** Location

### Decision

Lakeward is a protected wealthy enclave near Detroit.

It contains maintained roads, reliable utilities, private healthcare, schools, security, and supported technology.

Jonah and his family live there.

### Reason

Lakeward provides a physically close contrast to Eli’s neighborhood.

The social divide should be visible within driving distance.

### Consequences

Lakeward should feel comfortable and controlled, not fantastically futuristic.

Its residents believe their security is durable, but their access depends on corporate contracts.
