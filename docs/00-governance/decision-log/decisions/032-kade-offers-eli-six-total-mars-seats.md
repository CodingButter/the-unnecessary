## Decision 032: Kade Offers Eli Six Total Mars Seats

**Status:** Active but Revisable
**Category:** Plot

### Decision

Kade offers passage for Eli and five additional people.

### Reason

The number forces Eli to make the same kind of exclusionary choice as the Gatekeepers.

It is enough to save people he loves but not enough to preserve everyone important to him.

### Consequences

- Eli must mentally confront who he would choose.
- Jonah cannot fit his entire desired group comfortably alongside Eli’s relationships.
- The offer is genuinely valuable.
- Refusal carries real moral cost.

Later scene planning shows that another number creates a sharper dilemma.
