## Decision 037: The Tone Is Serious, Restrained, and Morally Ambiguous

**Status:** Locked for Current Draft
**Category:** Tone

### Decision

The novel should create slow-building dread, recognition, grief, anger, moral uncertainty, and fragile hope.

It should not become relentlessly bleak or emotionally manipulative.

### Reason

Ordinary humor, affection, beauty, and routine are necessary to show what is worth preserving.

### Consequences

- Villains should not explain their evil.
- The narration should trust readers to recognize contradictions.
- Emotional language should remain controlled.
- Hope must emerge from action and relationship, not motivational speeches.

### Affected Documents

- Narrative Brief
- Plot Outline
- Future Style Guide
- Chapter Blueprints

### Reconsider Only If

The intended reader experience changes.
