## Decision 033: Asterion Uses Pressure Before Open Force

**Status:** Locked for Current Draft
**Category:** Plot and institutional behavior

### Decision

Asterion responds through:

- Property claims
- licensing enforcement
- energy restrictions
- communications blocking
- medical credential pressure
- media framing
- government orders
- negotiated shutdown offers

Physical containment comes later.

### Reason

The corporation’s ability to dismantle a community bureaucratically is more plausible and frightening than an immediate armed assault.

### Consequences

- Sera’s operation begins as legal and technical containment.
- Kade can continue claiming reasonableness.
- The community becomes more dependent on Morrow as pressure grows.
- Violence occurs only after softer control fails.

### Affected Documents

- Story Bible
- Technology Rules
- Master Timeline
- Plot Outline

### Reconsider Only If

Specific enforcement methods change. The escalation pattern should remain.
