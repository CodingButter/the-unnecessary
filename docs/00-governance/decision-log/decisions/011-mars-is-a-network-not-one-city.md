## Decision 011: Mars Is a Network, Not One City

**Status:** Locked for Current Draft
**Category:** Mars worldbuilding

### Decision

The Aurelia Initiative consists of multiple connected settlement sites, industrial facilities, power fields, agricultural systems, mines, transportation routes, and underground habitats.

It is not one glass-domed city.

### Reason

A networked industrial project is more plausible and creates political and technical complexity.

Multiple corporate and national partners may control different parts.

### Consequences

- Mars cannot be disabled by one convenient switch.
- Different sites may develop different priorities.
- Separate Crown instances may operate with partial autonomy.
- Surface structures should mainly support power, transportation, communications, and industry.
- Human habitation is primarily underground or heavily shielded.

### Affected Documents

- Story Bible
- Technology Rules
- Master Timeline
- Later-series planning

### Reconsider Only If

A central administrative settlement develops later. It should remain part of the wider network.
