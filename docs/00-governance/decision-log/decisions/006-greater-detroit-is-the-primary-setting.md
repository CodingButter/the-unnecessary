## Decision 006: Greater Detroit Is the Primary Setting

**Status:** Active but Revisable
**Category:** Location

### Decision

Book One takes place primarily in Greater Detroit.

### Reason

The region supports the story through:

- Automotive and robotics history
- Industrial infrastructure
- Manufacturing knowledge
- Great Lakes water access
- Existing suburbs and cities
- Plausible abandoned research facilities
- Strong contrasts between wealthy enclaves and unsupported communities

Detroit also allows the story to explore a place that has already experienced cycles of industrial prosperity, decline, reinvestment, and abandonment.

### Consequences

Research should eventually confirm:

- Regional geography
- Travel times
- utility structure
- climate
- transportation
- industrial sites
- municipal relationships

### Affected Documents

- Story Bible
- Master Timeline
- Plot Outline
- Chapter Blueprints

### Reconsider Only If

Research reveals a major incompatibility or another setting provides a substantially stronger thematic and practical foundation.
