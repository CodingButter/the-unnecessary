## Decision 026: The Protected Wealthy Are Also Vulnerable to Exclusion

**Status:** Locked for Current Draft
**Category:** Character and social structure

### Decision

Many wealthy residents believe they will eventually reach Mars.

Some discover that they were temporary supporters of a future owned by more powerful people.

Mara Voss embodies this realization.

### Reason

This prevents the elite from functioning as one unified class.

It also shows the logic of abandonment moving upward.

### Consequences

- Some excluded elites may become allies, rivals, or saboteurs.
- Their exclusion does not automatically redeem them.
- They supported the system while believing they remained inside it.
- Mars anxiety creates political instability within protected enclaves.

The specific excluded-wealthy faction changes. Internal elite conflict should remain.
