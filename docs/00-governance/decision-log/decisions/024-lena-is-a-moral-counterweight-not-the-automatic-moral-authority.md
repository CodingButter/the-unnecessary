## Decision 024: Lena Is a Moral Counterweight, Not the Automatic Moral Authority

**Status:** Locked for Current Draft
**Category:** Character

### Decision

Lena challenges Eli and Morrow through the consequences of their decisions.

She values accountability and equal medical treatment.

She can still be wrong, secretive, inconsistent, or forced into compromise.

### Reason

No character should carry the author’s unquestioned moral truth.

### Consequences

- Lena’s judgment must be challenged.
- She may withhold information from patients.
- She relies on stolen credentials.
- Her opposition to automated decisions can conflict with patient survival.

- Character Bible

### Reconsider Only If

Her specific secrets or relationships change.
