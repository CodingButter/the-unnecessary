## Decision 027: Book One Takes Place in 2053

**Status:** Active but Revisable
**Category:** Timeline

### Decision

The opening date is Friday, October 3, 2053.

The final date is Saturday, November 1, 2053.

### Previous or Alternative Direction

The novel was initially considered for 2047.

### Reason

Moving the story to 2053 gives autonomous robotics and Martian construction more time to develop while keeping the future recognizable.

### Consequences

- Character ages are calculated from 2053.
- Mars construction begins in 2043.
- Crown has operated for approximately eighteen years.
- Eli has been outside Asterion for six years.

### Affected Documents

- Character Bible
- Master Timeline
- Plot Outline
- Chapter Blueprints

### Reconsider Only If

Research requires a timeline shift. Any shift must propagate through all ages, histories, and technology milestones.
