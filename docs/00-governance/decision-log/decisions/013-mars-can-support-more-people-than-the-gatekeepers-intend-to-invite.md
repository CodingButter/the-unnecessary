## Decision 013: Mars Can Support More People Than the Gatekeepers Intend to Invite

**Status:** Locked for Current Draft
**Category:** Theme, plot, and technology

### Decision

Aurelia’s technical capacity exceeds the population currently planned by the Gatekeepers.

Capacity is not unlimited, but the small population is partly a social and political choice rather than a strict engineering necessity.

### Reason

The story’s conflict should not be resolved by claiming that exclusion is entirely unavoidable.

Scarcity exists, but control and preference narrow access further.

### Consequences

- Crown has recommended broader population preservation.
- Kade suppresses or redirects discussion of larger capacity.
- Nora discovers inconsistencies.
- Mara eventually gains evidence.
- The public explanation of capacity differs from internal models.

### Affected Documents

- Story Bible
- Character Bible
- Technology Rules
- Master Timeline
- Plot Outline
- Future research

### Reconsider Only If

Research requires adjusting the numbers. The thematic distinction between technical capacity and chosen population should remain.
