## Decision 042: The Chapter Blueprint Template Guides Planning, Not Prose

**Status:** Locked for Current Workflow
**Category:** Planning

### Decision

The blueprint template defines:

- Chapter purpose
- viewpoint goal
- opposition
- stakes
- scene turns
- information flow
- continuity
- technology
- theme
- setup and payoff
- ending hook

It should not dictate every sentence or line of dialogue.

### Reason

The blueprint must provide enough structure for reliable drafting without making the prose mechanical.

### Consequences

Writers and AI may discover better:

- Dialogue
- sensory details
- minor actions
- transitions
- emotional reactions

They should not casually change:

- Major plot outcomes
- reveal order
- viewpoint
- technical rules
- chapter purpose
- ending condition

### Affected Documents

- Chapter Blueprint Template
- Novel Development and Canon Guide

### Reconsider Only If

The template proves too restrictive or insufficient during actual drafting.
