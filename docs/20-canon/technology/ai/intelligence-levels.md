# Levels of Artificial Intelligence

Not every system called AI has the same capabilities.

## Embedded Systems

Embedded systems perform narrow tasks inside appliances, vehicles, tools, medical equipment, and infrastructure.

Examples include:

- Battery management
- Motor control
- Object detection
- Equipment diagnostics
- Traffic routing
- Temperature control
- Medical monitoring

These systems are not conscious and cannot reason broadly.

Many continue functioning after corporate support ends, provided they do not require remote authentication.

## Specialized Agents

Specialized agents perform complex tasks within a limited field.

Examples include:

- Legal analysis
- Medical diagnosis
- Structural design
- Logistics planning
- Software engineering
- Financial modeling
- Agricultural management
- Security monitoring

They can communicate naturally and adapt within their field.

They do not possess broad independent goals unless connected to a larger coordinating intelligence.

## General Autonomous Systems

These systems can manage long-term objectives across multiple fields.

They can:

- Break goals into tasks
- Select tools
- Coordinate specialized agents
- Manage robots
- Request resources
- Revise plans
- Learn from failures

Most major corporations and governments have access to at least one general autonomous platform.

These systems are normally constrained by ownership, permissions, infrastructure, and institutional policies.

## Artificial Superintelligence

Superintelligence exceeds human capability across most cognitive fields.

It can:

- Develop new scientific theories
- Design technologies
- model economic and political systems
- coordinate enormous networks
- improve algorithms
- detect patterns humans miss
- evaluate millions of possible plans
- communicate persuasively with different audiences

Superintelligence is not omniscience.

It remains limited by available data, physical access, uncertainty, deliberate deception, computation, and conflicting objectives.

Crown is an established artificial superintelligence.

Morrow becomes an emerging one.
