# Northglass

## Facility History

Northglass was Asterion’s Great Lakes research and testing campus.

It included:

- Data centers
- Robotics laboratories
- industrial power systems
- autonomous-vehicle testing
- manufacturing prototypes
- medical-system research
- fiber infrastructure
- cooling plants
- underground service tunnels
- storage warehouses

## Why It Was Abandoned

Asterion relocated its most valuable operations to newer automated facilities.

Northglass remained because:

- Dismantling cost too much
- Some systems contained environmental hazards
- Ownership was legally complex
- Older equipment had limited corporate value
- Security systems made unauthorized access risky
- Asterion expected the property to remain inaccessible

## Remaining Technology

Northglass contains useful but inconsistent equipment.

Some systems work.

Some are damaged.

Some are traps created by automated security.

Some remain connected to Asterion.

Eli cannot assume recovered hardware is safe.
