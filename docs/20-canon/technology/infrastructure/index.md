# Infrastructure Technology Index

This directory holds the infrastructure systems of the world in "The Unnecessary": the physical and digital substrate that still runs, decays, or fails. Each file is the authority for its system. Energy is the primary constraint that gates the others.

Read first: [energy.md](energy.md), because energy availability governs what every other system can do.

| File | Summary | Authority | Load when |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [energy.md](energy.md) | The primary constraint: generation, load-shedding, and what fails first when power is scarce. | active-canon (technology) | Any scene where power, heat, or load-shedding matters |
| [communications.md](communications.md) | Networks, connectivity, and how communication degrades in abandoned areas. | active-canon (technology) | Scenes involving messaging, networks, or signal loss |
| [cloud-dependency.md](cloud-dependency.md) | Reliance on remote services and what breaks when the cloud withdraws. | active-canon (technology) | Scenes where a device or service stops working without the cloud |
| [identity-and-money.md](identity-and-money.md) | Identity, access, and money systems and their failure modes. | active-canon (technology) | Scenes involving payment, identity, or access control |
| [community-infrastructure.md](community-infrastructure.md) | Local systems communities preserve and run themselves. | active-canon (technology) | Scenes in self-sustaining neighborhoods |
| [computing-hardware.md](computing-hardware.md) | Processors, hardware longevity, data centers, and cooling. | active-canon (technology) | Questions about compute, hardware durability, or data centers |
