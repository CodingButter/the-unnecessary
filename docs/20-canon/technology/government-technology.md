# Government Technology

## Government Condition

Governments still exist and retain legal authority.

They depend heavily on private systems for:

- Tax collection
- identity
- security
- transportation
- power management
- communications
- healthcare
- public administration

This creates a gap between legal sovereignty and operational sovereignty.

## Automated Administration

Many government decisions are processed by AI.

Humans retain formal authority but often approve recommendations they cannot fully evaluate.

## Enforcement

Government systems may enforce corporate service decisions because the state lacks the infrastructure to provide alternatives.

This makes abandonment appear bureaucratic rather than openly political.
