The acceleration it describes is driven by the superintelligence detailed in [ai/crown.md](ai/crown.md), and the manufacturing and materials constraints that limit deployment are covered in [robotics-and-manufacturing.md](robotics-and-manufacturing.md).

# Scientific Progress

## Superintelligence Acceleration

Crown has accelerated progress in:

- Materials
- batteries
- medicine
- robotics
- energy systems
- climate modeling
- agriculture
- propulsion
- computation

## Limits on Deployment

A discovery still requires:

- Testing
- regulation
- manufacturing
- materials
- deployment
- maintenance
- public acceptance

Protected populations receive new technology quickly.

Ordinary communities often receive no new technology at all.

This creates a world where scientific progress continues while most people experience technological decline.
