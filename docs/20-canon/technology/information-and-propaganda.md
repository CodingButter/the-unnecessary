# Information and Propaganda

## Corporate Messaging

Asterion frames withdrawal as:

- Resource prioritization
- service consolidation
- risk management
- temporary restructuring
- infrastructure modernization
- regional transition

Language hides responsibility.

## Synthetic Media

AI-generated media is common.

Video and audio cannot be trusted automatically.

Authentication systems exist but are controlled by institutions that may have political interests.

## Reliable Evidence

Characters establish trust through:

- Local records
- multiple independent sources
- cryptographic signatures
- physical observation
- trusted human witnesses
- disconnected archives

Even verified evidence may be ignored if powerful institutions control distribution.
