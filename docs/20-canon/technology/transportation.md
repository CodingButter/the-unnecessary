# Transportation

## Autonomous Vehicles

Autonomous vehicles are mature.

They operate reliably on maintained roads with current maps and network support.

Performance declines in:

- Damaged roads
- Unmapped construction
- severe weather
- disconnected regions
- areas with altered signage
- environments containing unpredictable human traffic

Many unsupported communities use older vehicles in manual or reduced-autonomy modes.

## Freight

Long-distance freight is largely autonomous.

Major routes connecting protected regions remain maintained.

Routes serving unsupported populations become less frequent.

## Air Travel

Commercial air travel still exists but primarily serves protected regions, governments, corporations, and strategic operations.

Ordinary travel is expensive and restricted.

## Spaceflight

Launch systems are highly automated but remain physically expensive.

Each launch requires:

- Fuel
- vehicles
- maintenance
- launch windows
- weather conditions
- orbital coordination
- ground infrastructure

Moving people to Mars is significantly harder than sending machinery and cargo.

This helps explain why autonomous construction precedes migration.
