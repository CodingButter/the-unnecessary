## Mars

Mars appears through corporate footage, system telemetry, private meetings, and occasional viewpoint scenes connected to Kade and Crown.

The planet is inhabited primarily by machines.

Construction lights move across an empty landscape.

Excavators work without operators.

Underground habitats expand.

Agricultural chambers grow food for people who have not arrived.

The silence of Mars contrasts with the struggling human communities on Earth.
