# Eli's Kitchen

Where Eli sets the phone down on the counter and warms the kettle on the gas ring, and where the queued cellular-withdrawal notice arrives forwarded from the hub.

## Layout and Dimensions

A counter and a gas ring with a kettle, and a window. Eli sets the phone face down on the counter; "the phone was easy to set down. The notice was not."

## Contents and Furnishings

The counter holds the phone and the coffee things. Warmth comes from the gas ring. Eli "poured the water over yesterday's grounds because the new ones were a thing you paid for."

## Condition

Warmth is rationed to the gas ring; the home runs on stored power. Ordinary, kept ordinary by hand.
