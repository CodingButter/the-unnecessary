# Eli's Bedroom

The room where Chapter 1 opens. The phone wakes before Eli does, the screen pale in the morning light, the external bars showing nothing and only the mesh icon lit.

## Layout and Dimensions

A bed, and a window Eli carries the phone to, holding it up "a thing his father would have done with a transistor radio," to no effect. The bars do not come back.

## Light and Atmosphere

Pale morning light. "The room held the silence of a place running on stored power, the small outside noises a phone made when it was talking to the world all gone."

## Condition

The floor is cold underfoot when Eli puts his feet down. The room runs on stored battery power like the rest of the home.
