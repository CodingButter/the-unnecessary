# Webb

The only street named in the entire canon. It exists in the record through one thing: the corner of Webb, where a streetlight has been dead since spring.

## Character of the Street

An ordinary residential street in Eli's neighborhood, indistinguishable from any street in any year unless you know where to look. Eli reads it as he passes, the dead lights overhead "marking how far the maintenance had pulled back, block by block."

## Condition and Surface

Webb carries the neighborhood's withdrawn maintenance: at least one streetlight (on its named corner) is long dead, the glass gone milky. Canon names no length, no segments, and no cross-street, so none are recorded here.
