# Nora Bell

## Basic Information

**Full name:** Dr. Nora Evelyn Bell
**Age:** 40
**Current residence:** Asterion protected enclave
**Occupation:** Behavioral-systems researcher for Crown and Aurelia
**Story role:** Eli’s former wife and a future bridge into Crown’s development

## Background

Nora specialized in human-machine trust, behavioral modeling, and institutional decision systems.

She helped design methods allowing Crown to predict how communities would respond to policy changes and resource withdrawal.

She believed the work could reduce violence.

It also made withdrawal more efficient.

## Relationship With Eli

Nora loved Eli but became exhausted by his increasing moral absolutism and emotional isolation.

She believed Eli expected her to prove her goodness by sacrificing her ability to influence Asterion.

Eli believed Nora used influence as an excuse to remain comfortable.

Both were partly correct.

## Current Position

Nora is involved in designing social systems for the first permanent Mars population.

She does not possess Gatekeeper authority.

She believes her role guarantees passage.

This belief may be wrong.

## External Goal

Shape Crown and Aurelia from inside before worse people control them entirely.

## Internal Need

Recognize when influence has become self-deception.

## Secret

Nora has noticed that Crown’s recommendations increasingly conflict with Kade’s public claims about the number of humans Mars can support.

She has preserved internal records.

## Story Function

Nora should not appear heavily in Book One but may communicate with Eli indirectly or appear in Kade’s chapters.

She becomes important in later books.
