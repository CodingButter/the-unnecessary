# Celeste Mercer

## Basic Information

**Full name:** Celeste Anne Mercer
**Age:** 37
**Current residence:** Lakeward
**Occupation:** Former architect, currently manages Lakeward housing and resident services
**Story role:** Jonah’s moral and emotional counterweight

## Personality

Celeste is perceptive, controlled, loving, and less socially ambitious than Jonah.

She values Lakeward’s safety but does not believe the Gatekeepers owe her family anything.

She has spent years preparing quietly for the possibility that they will never reach Mars.

## Relationship With Jonah

Celeste loves Jonah but sees how fear shapes him.

She repeatedly asks him to build a future they can control rather than chasing an invitation.

Jonah interprets this as resignation.

Celeste interprets Jonah’s ambition as dependency.

## External Goal

Protect her children without allowing their future to depend entirely on powerful strangers.

## Secret

Celeste has been transferring resources and technical supplies to a rural community where her sister lives.

She has considered leaving Lakeward.

## Book One Function

Celeste recognizes Jonah’s involvement with Morrow before he confesses.

Her reaction forces him to see that his betrayal was not an act universally supported by the family he claimed to protect.
