# Book One Chapter-Map Index

These are high-level plot-map entries, distinct from chapter blueprints. Each entry carries only the chapter's high-level outline: purpose, movement, character movement, information revealed, and ending hook. The detailed scene-by-scene plan for a chapter lives in its chapter blueprint under the blueprints layer, not here.

## Read first

When you are planning a chapter, read that chapter's plot-map entry below first, then read its chapter blueprint. The plot-map entry gives you the structural shape; the blueprint gives you the detailed plan. For canonical facts about characters, technology, locations, and dates, defer to the canon authorities linked inside each entry rather than restating them here.
