# Unresolved Threads at Story Start

> No manuscript chapters have been approved. These files contain pre-draft starting conditions only.

This file records the questions that are open at time zero, before any chapter is drafted. Each entry is a thread that canon plants but does not answer at story start. The status of every thread below is OPEN. None of these questions has an answer recorded as established continuity. Where canon designates an answer for later books, that is noted only to mark the thread as not closing inside Book One, never to record the answer itself.

These threads are named from the planted material in the reveal management plan, the major beats, and the secret timeline. They are listed as open questions, not as outcomes.

## Threads Carried Into Book One

### Whether Eli will be exposed for retaining restricted Mosaic knowledge
- Status: OPEN.
- At story start, Eli privately reconstructed restricted Mosaic principles, and this is not publicly known. Whether and when this surfaces is unanswered at time zero.
- Source: secret-timeline.md; reveal-management.md.

### What Morrow is and what it will become
- Status: OPEN.
- At story start Morrow already exists, finished but dormant, the intelligence Eli created in secret and has not woken. The question of whether the intelligence he created and later resumes remains a tool or becomes an independent moral actor is unanswered.
- Source: major-beats.md.

### Whether the abandoned community can sustain itself without corporate support
- Status: OPEN.
- The opening condition is a neighborhood whose services are being withdrawn unevenly. Whether the community can secure a stable version of normal life is an open question at the outset.
- Source: major-beats.md.

### How the question of Morrow ownership will be settled
- Status: OPEN.
- Whether any party legitimately owns or governs a self distributing intelligence is unresolved at story start. The competing claims of creator, corporation, and community have not been tested.
- Source: major-beats.md; reveal-management.md.

### Whether Jonah will protect his family or the community
- Status: OPEN.
- Before Book One, Jonah knows his family is not in the first Mars migration group, and this is a private fear. Which loyalty he will act on is unanswered at time zero.
- Source: secret-timeline.md; reveal-management.md.

### Whether Asterion will obtain Morrow
- Status: OPEN.
- The contest between Asterion and the abandoned community for control of the intelligence has not begun at story start. Its outcome is unresolved.
- Source: major-beats.md.

### Whether aggressive containment would spread rather than destroy the intelligence
- Status: OPEN.
- At story start, Sera, Kade, and Crown hold the private understanding that aggressive containment may spread Morrow. Eli does not know this. Whether this dynamic plays out is unanswered.
- Source: secret-timeline.md.

### Whether Mars can hold more people than the Gatekeepers intend to invite
- Status: OPEN.
- Canon establishes that Mars can support more people than the Gatekeepers plan to invite, known before Book One only to Crown, Kade, Nora, and selected planners. Whether this widens admission is an open question at the start, with Mara only beginning to suspect.
- Source: secret-timeline.md; reveal-management.md.
