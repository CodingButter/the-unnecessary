# Relationship State at Story Start: Jonah and Celeste

> No manuscript chapters have been approved. These files contain pre-draft starting conditions only.

## Baseline classification

Married. Loving but strained. Canon: love strained by Jonah's belief that he alone must secure the family's future.

## History to story start

Jonah is married to Celeste Mercer. They live in Lakeward and have two children, Julian, age twelve, and Amelia, age eight. Celeste is a former architect who currently manages Lakeward housing and resident services.

## Trust and tension

Celeste understands the Mercer family's position more clearly than Jonah does. She does not trust Asterion's vague promises and believes Jonah confuses being invited into private rooms with being respected by the people inside them. She repeatedly asks Jonah to build a future they can control rather than chasing an invitation; Jonah interprets this as resignation, and Celeste interprets Jonah's ambition as dependency. Celeste understands that the pursuit of elite approval may destroy the relationships Jonah claims to protect. The marriage is loving but strained by Jonah's refusal to admit how little control he possesses.

## Asymmetry of knowledge at story start

At story start Jonah is carrying an undisclosed fact: a private Asterion contact warned him that later Mars migration waves would be smaller than publicly implied, and Jonah did not tell Celeste or his father. This concealment is already true at time zero. Celeste, separately, has been quietly transferring resources and technical supplies to a rural community where her sister lives and has considered leaving Lakeward; canon does not state Jonah knows this.

## Alliance and debt

A family alliance under strain. Both are committed to protecting their children, but they disagree on method: Jonah pursues Gatekeeper approval and Mars passage, Celeste prepares quietly for a future that does not depend on powerful strangers.

## Last known interaction baseline

Canon does not fix a single most recent interaction. The marriage is established as ongoing and cohabiting at story start.

## Planned, not yet occurred

Per canon, the following are Book One arc developments and are NOT established at story start. Named only to prevent confusion with baseline facts.

- Celeste recognizing Jonah's involvement with Morrow before he confesses (PLANNED, not yet delivered). Canon: she is not initially aware that Jonah exposes Morrow.
- Celeste's reaction of anger rather than gratitude when she learns the truth (PLANNED, not yet delivered).
