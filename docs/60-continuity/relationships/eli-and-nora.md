# Relationship State at Story Start: Eli and Nora

> No manuscript chapters have been approved. These files contain pre-draft starting conditions only.

## Baseline classification

Former spouses, divorced and estranged. This pairing is not in the relationship map but is established in Eli's profile and is significant to his starting state.

## History to story start

Eli was previously married to Nora Bell, an Asterion behavioral-systems researcher. Their marriage deteriorated as Eli became increasingly withdrawn and morally conflicted. Nora believed the transformation could no longer be stopped and that remaining inside Asterion was the only way to influence it. Eli believed staying made them collaborators. Their final argument occurred after Eli rejected Kade's offer. Nora remained with Asterion.

## Status at story start

They have been divorced for six years when Book One begins. They have no children. Nora remains alive and, per canon, may become important in later books. Eli rarely speaks about her. He still keeps an old analog photograph of the two of them from before either worked at Asterion.

## Trust and tension

The relationship is severed but not emotionally neutral. The retained photograph indicates unresolved attachment on Eli's side. Their split was ideological as much as personal, dividing on whether to stay inside Asterion or refuse it.

## Last known interaction baseline

The last fixed interaction in canon is their final argument, which occurred after Eli rejected Kade's offer, roughly six years before story start.

## Planned, not yet occurred

- Nora's possible importance in later books is flagged in canon but is outside Book One and not part of the starting baseline.
