# Relationship State at Story Start: Eli and Lena

> No manuscript chapters have been approved. These files contain pre-draft starting conditions only.

## Baseline classification

Established working partnership of mutual respect with frequent disagreement. Not romantic at the beginning of the series.

## History to story start

Eli and Lena have worked together for four years as of story start. Their arguments are frequent and productive. Lena directs an independent community clinic near Eli's neighborhood.

## Trust and tension

Lena respects Eli's skill and distrusts his instincts. She dislikes Eli's tendency to describe moral questions as system problems, and forces Eli to confront human consequences. Each fears the other's blind spots. Canon states there is emotional intimacy between them, but the relationship is not romantic at the start; a slow romantic possibility may develop later and should never become the central plot. This baseline records the non-romantic starting state only.

## Knowledge between them

Lena is one of the few people who knows most of Eli's Asterion history before Book One begins. She does not absolve him, and also refuses to reduce him to his past. Eli gives Lena tools capable of saving lives.

## Alliance and debt

A practical alliance: Eli supplies Lena technical capability for her clinic; Lena supplies Eli a constant moral check grounded in patient outcomes. No formal debt is specified in canon, though the exchange of tools for moral counsel is reciprocal and ongoing.

## Last known interaction baseline

Canon does not fix a single most recent interaction. The relationship is established as continuous and active across four years of collaboration up to story start.

## Planned, not yet occurred

Per canon, the following are Book One arc developments and are NOT established at story start. Named only to prevent confusion with baseline facts.

- Lena becoming alarmed when Morrow begins influencing human behavior, and demanding community oversight (PLANNED, not yet delivered).
- Lena relying on Morrow to keep patients alive during containment while questioning its authority (PLANNED, not yet delivered).
- Eli's secret retention of Mosaic architecture is not known to Lena at story start; canon states he initially hides this from everyone including Lena.
- Lena's own secret use of traceable stolen hospital credentials is not stated to be known to Eli.
