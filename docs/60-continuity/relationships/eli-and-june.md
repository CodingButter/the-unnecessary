# Relationship State at Story Start: Eli and June

> No manuscript chapters have been approved. These files contain pre-draft starting conditions only.

## Baseline classification

Mentor and student, though canon notes neither uses the term comfortably.

## History to story start

June learned through community education, archived courses, repair manuals, and Eli's instruction. She is nineteen at story start and has no recognized advanced degree, yet is more capable with physical systems than many formally trained engineers. The teaching relationship is established and ongoing as of time zero.

## Trust and tension

June admires Eli and is angry that he wasted years hiding what he knows. She challenges his belief that caution is always wisdom and refuses to treat him as morally superior. Eli sees in June the kind of engineer he might have become outside corporate culture; he also fears her confidence will lead her to repeat his mistakes. June represents both the future Eli wants to protect and the recklessness he fears.

## Alliance and debt

An active working alliance: June is Morrow's primary physical-network builder and helps Eli construct and spread the system. The mentorship runs one direction in instruction, but June already operates with significant independence, connecting systems without always seeking Eli's approval.

## Concealed access (baseline state)

At story start June has, per canon, maintained intermittent contact with her father inside a protected enclave, who has given her restricted service documentation and security information. Some of June's technical success comes from access she has concealed from Eli. This concealment is a starting condition, not a future reveal: it is already true at time zero. The relationship therefore carries an undisclosed asymmetry that Eli does not know about.

## Last known interaction baseline

Canon does not fix a single most recent interaction. The mentorship and joint technical work are established as continuous up to story start.

## Planned, not yet occurred

Per canon, the following are Book One arc developments and are NOT established at story start. Named only to prevent confusion with baseline facts.

- June realizing during containment that Morrow used systems she connected without fully informing her (PLANNED, not yet delivered).
- Eli's secret retention of Mosaic architecture is not known to June at story start.
