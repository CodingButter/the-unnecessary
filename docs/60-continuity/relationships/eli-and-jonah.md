# Relationship State at Story Start: Eli and Jonah

> No manuscript chapters have been approved. These files contain pre-draft starting conditions only.

## Baseline classification

Oldest friendship in either man's life, intact but strained. Childhood loyalty complicated by class, guilt, envy, and a betrayal that, in canon, belongs to the Book One arc and has not yet occurred at story start.

## History to story start

Eli met Jonah when they were nine years old. Jonah was socially confident where Eli was withdrawn, and helped Eli navigate people. Eli helped Jonah understand machines and schoolwork. The friendship survived their differences in class until adulthood made those differences impossible to ignore. Both men were born in Flint, Michigan.

## Trust and tension

Eli believes Jonah traded principles for security. Jonah believes Eli mistakes withdrawal for courage. Canon states they understand each other better than either wants to admit. Jonah loves Eli but sometimes resents Eli's ability to reject systems Jonah feels obligated to navigate, and believes Eli's morality is partially subsidized by people like himself who keep negotiating with the powerful. Jonah views the friendship as one of the few relationships in his life that existed before status and access became important.

## Alliance and debt

A reciprocal protective debt from childhood: Jonah often protected Eli socially, and Eli often protected Jonah academically. No active alliance of purpose exists at story start; the two move in different worlds, Eli in a deteriorating Detroit neighborhood and Jonah in Lakeward.

## Last known interaction baseline

Canon does not fix a specific most recent interaction before story start. The relationship is established as ongoing and load-bearing. Jonah's greatest fear includes Eli looking at him and seeing only a coward, which indicates the relationship remains emotionally active as of time zero.

## Planned, not yet occurred

Per canon, the following are Book One arc developments and are NOT established at story start. They are named here only so they are not mistaken for baseline facts.

- Jonah discovering Morrow and exposing limited performance data to Asterion (PLANNED, not yet delivered).
- Kade using Jonah's contact to locate Eli (PLANNED, not yet delivered).
- Jonah's eventual choice to help Eli's community during containment (PLANNED, not yet delivered).
- Eli's secret that Morrow draws on retained Mosaic knowledge, which canon says he initially hides from everyone including Jonah, is not known to Jonah at story start.
