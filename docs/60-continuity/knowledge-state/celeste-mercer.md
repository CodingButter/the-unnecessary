# Knowledge State: Celeste Mercer (Story Start)

> No manuscript chapters have been approved. These files contain pre-draft starting conditions only.

This file records what Celeste Mercer knows, privately holds, and does not yet know at story start, the morning of Friday, October 3, 2053. It is derived strictly from approved canon. No later event is recorded here as already known.

## What She Knows

- She lives in Lakeward, is a former architect, and currently manages Lakeward housing and resident services.
- She values Lakeward's safety but does not believe the Gatekeepers owe her family anything.
- She has spent years quietly preparing for the possibility that her family will never reach Mars.
- She understands that her husband Jonah's pursuit of elite approval may threaten the relationships he claims to protect, and she repeatedly asks him to build a future they can control rather than chasing an invitation.

## What She Privately Holds at Story Start

- Celeste has been transferring resources and technical supplies to a rural community where her sister lives, and she has considered leaving Lakeward. Per canon this is her secret and it is in motion at story start.

## What She Falsely Believes

- Canon does not assign Celeste an explicit false belief. None is recorded here.

## What She Does Not Yet Know

- She does not yet know with certainty that the Mercer family is excluded from the first permanent migration group. Canon establishes that Jonah received this confirmation in August 2053 and hid it from his family, so it is information Jonah holds and Celeste learns during Book One, not at story start.
- She does not yet know about Jonah's involvement with Morrow. Canon establishes that her recognition of his involvement, before he confesses, is a Book One function that has not occurred at story start.
- She does not yet know the eventual outcome of the family's Mars prospects, which remains unresolved at story start.
