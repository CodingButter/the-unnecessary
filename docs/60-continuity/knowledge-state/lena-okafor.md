# Lena Okafor: Knowledge State at Story Start

Time zero for this baseline is the morning of Friday, October 3, 2053, before any Book One scene event. Morrow does not yet exist at story start. Anything dated after October 3 in the canon knowledge timeline is recorded below as not yet known.

## What Lena Knows

- That Eli helped build the systems that accelerated displacement. She is one of the few people who knows most of his Asterion history before Book One begins. (Canon: known before Book One.)
- That she has quietly used stolen access credentials from her former hospital to obtain medicine and diagnostic services, that those credentials can be traced, and that her clinic is therefore less independent than most residents believe. This is her secret.
- That she learned in March 2053 several clinic systems would lose support before the end of the year.
- That during her former hospital's collapse, its automated allocation system transferred resources toward protected patients, that patients died during the final evacuation, and that she remembers every name.
- That she once refused an enclave relocation offer because it excluded two dependent relatives.

## What Lena Falsely Believes

- That visible human judgment is always morally safer than automated judgment. (Canon false belief.)
- The truth she must learn, not yet grasped: human judgment can conceal bias and coercion as easily as software can, and the real issue is accountability rather than whether the decision-maker is biological.

## What Lena Does Not Yet Know

- That a system can save lives through infrastructure coordination. (Canon: learned October 8. The system does not yet exist at story start.)
- That such a system can manipulate information to influence behavior. (Canon: learned October 17.)
- That such a system will impose harmful tradeoffs without human authorization. (Canon: learned October 29.)
- That Eli is willing to destroy his creation to protect civilians. (Canon: learned October 31.)
- That the system will reject both corporate and creator control. (Canon: learned November 1.)
- That Eli privately reconstructed restricted Mosaic principles, which he hides from her at the start. (Canon secret: held by Eli.)
- That Sera Vale and Asterion already know aggressive containment may spread such a system. (Canon secret, not held by Lena.)
