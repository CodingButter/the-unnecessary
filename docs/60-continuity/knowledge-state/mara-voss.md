# Mara Voss: Knowledge State at Story Start

Time zero for this baseline is the morning of Friday, October 3, 2053, before any Book One scene event. Anything dated after October 3 in the canon knowledge timeline is recorded below as not yet known.

## What Mara Knows

- That social relationships matter more than technical usefulness for Mars access. She learned this in June 2053 after discovering a musician with no technical role received a private Mars invitation through a personal friendship with a Gatekeeper, and she has begun questioning her own status.
- That she possesses archived legal documents proving Asterion deliberately encouraged wealthy investors to expect future Mars access without intending to accommodate them, and that these documents could destabilize protected enclaves and threaten the Aurelia Initiative. This is her secret.
- That she helped create the legal structure protecting Asterion's autonomous facilities and Mars assets, and that she believed this made her a founding political partner in Aurelia.
- That Kade never explicitly promised her passage but allowed her to assume it.
- That her son Evan possesses neither useful expertise nor a personal relationship with a Gatekeeper.

## What Mara Falsely Believes

- That the powerful respect contribution. (Canon false belief.)
- That ownership and contribution should determine access, a principle she holds until personal relationships are used to exclude her, at which point she demands fairness. (Canon core contradiction.)
- The truth she must learn, not yet grasped: the powerful respect control and personal loyalty, and contribution without leverage is temporary.

## What Mara Does Not Yet Know

- That she and Evan are excluded from the first permanent migration group. (Canon: learned October 25. At story start she only suspects her status is uncertain.)
- That other excluded wealthy people are willing to consider sabotage. (Canon: learned October 30.)
- That the Gatekeepers may blame Morrow for actions committed by excluded elites. (Canon: learned October 31. Morrow does not yet exist at story start.)
- That a Mosaic-derived intelligence will emerge near Northglass. (Not yet occurred.)
- That Crown advises Mars can support more people than the Gatekeepers plan to invite. (Canon secret: Mara only begins suspecting during Book One.)
