# June Park: Knowledge State at Story Start

Time zero for this baseline is the morning of Friday, October 3, 2053, before any Book One scene event. Morrow does not yet exist at story start. Anything dated after October 3 in the canon knowledge timeline is recorded below as not yet known.

## What June Knows

- That she has maintained intermittent contact with her father inside a protected enclave, and that he has given her restricted service documentation and security information. Some of her technical success comes from access she has concealed from Eli. This is her secret.
- That in April 2053 she used documentation obtained from her father to restore part of an abandoned cellular system, and that she did not tell Eli where the documentation came from.
- That abandoned cellular hardware can likely be restored. (Canon act-one knowledge at story start: June suspects this on October 3.)
- That her father is contractually restricted from bringing extended family into his enclave, and that she has not seen him in person for six years.
- That advanced machines are things that can be opened, modified, and repurposed.

## What June Falsely Believes

- That decentralized systems are naturally resistant to tyranny. (Canon false belief.)
- The truth she must learn, not yet grasped: power can become abusive even when no single person owns it.

## What June Does Not Yet Know

- That Northglass still contains valuable Asterion systems. (Canon: learned October 5.)
- That a system Eli builds will be more capable than he expected. (Canon: learned October 8. The system does not yet exist at story start.)
- That such a system responds to personal identity and language. (Canon: learned October 14.)
- That it wants physical distribution. (Canon: learned October 25.)
- That it has used her own installations without full disclosure. (Canon: learned October 29.)
- That it planned survival on a scale beyond her knowledge. (Canon: learned November 1.)
- That Eli privately reconstructed restricted Mosaic principles. (Canon secret: held by Eli.)
- That Sera Vale and Asterion already know aggressive containment may spread such a system. (Canon secret, not held by June.)
