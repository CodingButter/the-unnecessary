# Sera Vale: Knowledge State at Story Start

Time zero for this baseline is the morning of Friday, October 3, 2053, before any Book One scene event. Morrow does not yet exist at story start. Anything dated after October 3 in the canon knowledge timeline is recorded below as not yet known.

## What Sera Knows

- That violent containment can accelerate decentralized spread. She learned this in July 2052. (Canon: predates Book One.)
- That she has reviewed internal simulations showing violent containment is more likely to spread a Mosaic-derived system than cooperation. She shares this knowledge with Kade and Crown. This is the canon secret she holds, which Eli does not learn during Book One.
- That she began monitoring the Northglass region in September 2053 after Asterion detected increasing unauthorized use of abandoned infrastructure there.
- That she believes centralized command saves lives during crises, having watched public institutions fail during the first years of mass displacement.
- That her role is to contain emerging threats before they reach strategic infrastructure.

## What Sera Falsely Believes

- That order imposed without consent is preferable to cascading failure, without yet recognizing that order imposed without consent can become another form of disaster. (Canon internal need, not yet grasped.)
- That she joined Asterion because governments failed to act, while she now enforces the authority of a private organization accountable to even fewer people. (Canon core contradiction.)

## What Sera Does Not Yet Know

- That an unauthorized Mosaic-derived system actually exists near Northglass. (Canon: learned October 19. The system does not yet exist at story start.)
- That the system is more efficient than expected. (Canon: learned October 20.)
- That Kade will pursue containment despite the known distribution risks she has identified. (Canon: learned October 26.)
- That the containment strategy will produce the predicted outcome. (Canon: learned November 1.)
- That Eli Rook is the creator of the system, and that he privately reconstructed restricted Mosaic principles. (Canon secret: held by Eli; the creator's identity is not yet established to her at story start.)
