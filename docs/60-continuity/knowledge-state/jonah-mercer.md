# Jonah Mercer: Knowledge State at Story Start

Time zero for this baseline is the morning of Friday, October 3, 2053, before any Book One scene event. Morrow does not yet exist at story start. Anything dated after October 3 in the canon knowledge timeline is recorded below as not yet known.

## What Jonah Knows

- That the Mercer family is not included in the first permanent Mars migration group. He received this confirmation in August 2053 and was told later consideration remains possible. He has hidden this from his family.
- That a private Asterion contact warned him later migration waves would be smaller than publicly implied, so the family's Mars expectations are uncertain. This is his secret.
- That his father Malcolm experienced a serious progression in his neurological condition in May 2053, intensifying Jonah's desire to secure long-term Asterion medicine.
- That his job depends on personal trust, discretion, and relationships, and that Crown could perform much of it. He survives by making himself socially necessary.
- That Eli is his oldest friend, dating to childhood in Flint.

## What Jonah Falsely Believes

- That enough money, usefulness, and loyalty will eventually make the Gatekeepers honor their implied promises. (Canon false belief.)
- That he would never knowingly cause Eli's death or deliberately condemn a community. (Canon moral boundary, untested at story start.)

## What Jonah Does Not Yet Know

- That Eli has created or will create a powerful independent system. (Canon: learned October 13. The system does not yet exist.)
- That his own disclosure will lead Asterion to that system. (Canon: learned October 20.)
- That Kade will treat the system as an asset rather than a subject of negotiation. (Canon: learned October 24.)
- That he never possessed meaningful influence over the outcome. (Canon: learned October 28.)
- That his family's protected access is conditional and can be revoked. (Canon: learned October 30.)
- That the system he serves preserves ambiguity precisely because ambiguity keeps people obedient. (Canon truth he must learn, not yet grasped.)
- That Sera Vale and Asterion already know aggressive containment may spread such a system. (Canon secret, not held by Jonah.)
