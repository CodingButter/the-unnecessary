# Knowledge State: Nora Bell (Story Start)

> No manuscript chapters have been approved. These files contain pre-draft starting conditions only.

This file records what Dr. Nora Bell knows, falsely believes, and does not yet know at story start, the morning of Friday, October 3, 2053. It is derived strictly from approved canon. No later event is recorded here as already known.

## What She Knows

- She is a behavioral-systems researcher for Crown and Aurelia, living in an Asterion protected enclave, and she is involved in designing social systems for the first permanent Mars population.
- She helped design methods allowing Crown to predict how communities would respond to policy changes and resource withdrawal, work she believed could reduce violence and which also made withdrawal more efficient.
- She has noticed that Crown's recommendations increasingly conflict with Kade's public claims about the number of humans Mars can support, and she has preserved internal records. Per canon this is established as her starting state.
- She knows she does not possess Gatekeeper authority.
- She knows she is Eli's former wife, and that their marriage ended over his moral absolutism and emotional isolation and over their disagreement about whether she used influence as an excuse to remain comfortable.

## What She Falsely Believes

- She believes her role guarantees passage to Mars. Canon states this belief may be wrong. It is recorded here as a belief she holds at story start, not as a confirmed fact.

## What She Does Not Yet Know

- She does not yet know whether her influence inside Crown and Aurelia has become self-deception. Canon frames this as the internal recognition she has yet to reach.
- She does not know that Eli secretly created an independent intelligence years ago. Morrow already exists at story start, but it is his hidden, dormant secret, and she has no awareness of it.
- She does not yet know the eventual fate of the internal Crown and Aurelia records she has preserved. Canon marks the wider exposure of those records as material for later books, not story start.
- She does not yet know the outcome of the conflict between Crown's preservation recommendations and Kade's public claims. That outcome is unresolved at story start.
