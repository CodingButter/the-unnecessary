# Character State: Mara Voss (Story Start)

> No manuscript chapters have been approved. These files contain pre-draft starting conditions only.

This file records what is true about Mara Voss as of Book One time zero. It derives strictly from the approved canon profile and the pre-book timeline. Arc outcomes and planned beats are listed separately and marked PLANNED; they are not part of her known or established state at story start.

## Location

Lakeward, a protected enclave. Birthplace Grand Rapids, Michigan.

## Physical Condition

- Age 56. Tall, elegant, carefully composed.
- Blonde hair beginning to gray; she refuses aggressive age-reversal treatments because she believes visible age communicates authority.
- Clothing formal even in private environments. She controls her expressions in public and allows anger to appear only through stillness.

## Resource Condition

- Former Governor of Michigan; now a strategic adviser, investor, and political broker.
- Holds protected Lakeward status and significant political and financial leverage.
- Her continued access to elite protection, and specifically Mars passage, is not contractually guaranteed.

## Immediate Situation

- Rose through state politics during the infrastructure crisis and negotiated agreements allowing Asterion and other corporations to assume control over power, transportation, and security systems. Her decisions preserved some services and also accelerated the abandonment of unprofitable communities. Many people outside protected districts consider her a collaborator.
- Helped create the legal structure protecting Asterion's autonomous facilities and Mars assets, and believed this made her a founding political partner in Aurelia. Kade never explicitly promised her passage; he allowed her to assume it.
- Divorced. One adult son, Evan Voss, age twenty-seven, who lives carelessly inside Lakeward and assumes Mars access is guaranteed. Mara's fear intensifies as she registers that Evan possesses neither useful expertise nor a personal relationship with a Gatekeeper.
- In June 2053 Mara discovered that a musician with no technical role had received a private Mars invitation through a personal friendship with a Gatekeeper, and she began questioning her own status.

## Active Goals (as of story start)

- External: secure Mars passage for herself and her son.

## Stated Secrets (held, not yet revealed)

- Mara possesses archived legal documents proving that Asterion deliberately encouraged wealthy investors to expect future Mars access without intending to accommodate them. These documents could destabilize protected enclaves and threaten the Aurelia Initiative.

## Known State Caveats

- Mara's greatest fear is becoming irrelevant while still alive to witness it. This is a fear, not a fact about events.
- She holds the false belief that the powerful respect contribution.
- Her core contradiction: she believes ownership and contribution should determine access until personal relationships are used to exclude her, after which she demands fairness.

## PLANNED (not established at story start; do not record as occurred)

The following are canon arc projections, not facts true at time zero. They must not be treated as known to Mara or as having happened:

- Mara begins confident in her future, discovers that less influential people received invitations through personal relationships, and quietly investigates; her fear turns into anger.
- By the end of Book One she is connected to a faction considering sabotage.
- She does not become a revolutionary; she becomes a powerful person trying to survive exclusion.
