# Celeste Mercer: Character State at Story Start

> No manuscript chapters have been approved. These files contain pre-draft starting conditions only.

This file records what is true about Celeste Mercer at time zero, the opening of Book One. It derives strictly from approved canon and records no planned or future event as established fact.

## Identity and Location

Celeste Anne Mercer is thirty-seven years old and lives in Lakeward, a protected enclave.

She is a former architect who currently manages Lakeward housing and resident services.

## Condition and Disposition

Celeste is perceptive, controlled, loving, and less socially ambitious than her partner Jonah. She values Lakeward's safety but does not believe the Gatekeepers owe her family anything.

Canon records no injury or illness for her at story start.

## Immediate Situation

Celeste lives inside Lakeward while her household carries growing pressure tied to Asterion and the Mars migration. In the months before Book One, Jonah's father Malcolm experienced a serious progression in a neurological condition, and Jonah grew increasingly desperate to secure long-term access to Asterion medicine.

In August 2053 Jonah received confirmation that the Mercer family is not in the first permanent migration group and hid this from his family. At story start Celeste has not been told this. She does not yet know that the family was excluded from the first group.

She has spent years quietly preparing for the possibility that they will never reach Mars.

## Active Goals

Her external goal is to protect her children without allowing their future to depend entirely on powerful strangers.

She repeatedly asks Jonah to build a future they can control rather than chasing an invitation. Jonah interprets this as resignation, and she interprets his ambition as dependency.

## Held Secret at Story Start

Celeste has been transferring resources and technical supplies to a rural community where her sister lives, and she has considered leaving Lakeward. At time zero this preparation is hers alone and is not disclosed.

## Not Recorded as Occurred (PLANNED, per canon, not yet delivered)

The following are canon-planned developments and are NOT part of Celeste's state at time zero:

- Recognizing Jonah's involvement with Morrow before he confesses.
- Learning that the family is not in the first migration group.
- Any decision to leave Lakeward.
