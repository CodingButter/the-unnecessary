# Character State: Sera Vale (Story Start)

> No manuscript chapters have been approved. These files contain pre-draft starting conditions only.

This file records what is true about Sera Vale as of Book One time zero. It derives strictly from the approved canon profile and the pre-book timeline. Arc outcomes and planned beats are listed separately and marked PLANNED; they are not part of her known or established state at story start.

## Location

Asterion mobile command facilities. Birthplace Toronto, Canada.

## Physical Condition

- Age 44. Average height with closely cropped dark hair.
- Has a prosthetic left leg below the knee, the result of an infrastructure riot earlier in her career.
- Wears understated Asterion field clothing rather than a military uniform.

## Resource Condition

- Director of Asterion Continuity Security.
- Commands Asterion operational resources and authority granted by the company, which recruited her by offering resources and operational authority.

## Immediate Situation

- Previously worked in public emergency management and coordinated disaster relief during the first years of mass displacement. She watched public institutions fail because of corruption, indecision, and political interference, and believes centralized command saves lives during crises.
- Respects Eli's competence and views his refusal to cooperate as irresponsible.
- In September 2053 Asterion detected increasing unauthorized use of abandoned infrastructure around Northglass, and Sera began monitoring the region.

## Active Goals (as of story start)

- External: contain Morrow before it spreads into strategic infrastructure. (At time zero this is her stated charge; the active containment operation against Morrow is a Book One arc beat, not a completed action. Her September 2053 activity is regional monitoring of unauthorized infrastructure use around Northglass.)

## Stated Secrets (held, not yet revealed)

- Sera has reviewed internal simulations showing that violent containment is more likely to spread Morrow than cooperation. Kade orders containment anyway because allowing Morrow to remain independent would create a political precedent. (The containment order itself is a Book One arc development; the secret recorded here is Sera's knowledge of the simulation findings.)

## Known State Caveats

- Sera's greatest fear is uncontrolled cascading failure; she sees distributed systems as dangerous because no single person can stop them. This is a fear, not a fact about events.
- Her core contradiction: she joined Asterion because governments failed to act, and now enforces the authority of a private organization accountable to even fewer people.
- Her moral boundary: she will not intentionally order lethal force against civilians unless she believes immediate mass casualties will otherwise occur.

## PLANNED (not established at story start; do not record as occurred)

The following are canon arc projections, not facts true at time zero. They must not be treated as known to Sera or as having happened beyond her time-zero monitoring activity:

- Sera becomes the operational antagonist responsible for containing Morrow once a containment operation is ordered.
