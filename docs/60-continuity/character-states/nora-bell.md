# Nora Bell: Character State at Story Start

> No manuscript chapters have been approved. These files contain pre-draft starting conditions only.

This file records what is true about Dr. Nora Bell at time zero, the opening of Book One. It derives strictly from approved canon and records no planned or future event as established fact.

## Identity and Location

Dr. Nora Evelyn Bell is forty years old and lives in an Asterion protected enclave.

She is a behavioral-systems researcher for Crown and Aurelia. She specialized in human-machine trust, behavioral modeling, and institutional decision systems, and she helped design methods allowing Crown to predict how communities would respond to policy changes and resource withdrawal.

She is Eli Rook's former wife.

## Condition and Disposition

Canon records no injury or illness for Nora at story start. She lives inside the relative security of an Asterion protected enclave.

She believed her work could reduce violence, while it also made withdrawal more efficient.

## Immediate Situation

At story start Nora is involved in designing social systems for the first permanent Mars population. She does not possess Gatekeeper authority.

Canon places her role away from the immediate Greater Detroit crisis. Her story function notes she should not appear heavily in Book One and may communicate with Eli indirectly or appear in Kade's chapters. Nothing in canon records direct contact between Nora and Eli as having occurred at time zero.

## Active Goals

Her external goal is to shape Crown and Aurelia from inside before worse people control them entirely.

Her internal need, unmet at story start, is to recognize when influence has become self-deception.

## False Belief at Story Start

Nora believes her role guarantees passage to Mars. Canon states this belief may be wrong. It is recorded here as her starting belief, not as a resolved outcome.

## Held Secret at Story Start

Nora has noticed that Crown's recommendations increasingly conflict with Kade's public claims about the number of humans Mars can support, and she has preserved internal records. At time zero this observation and the preserved records are hers and are not disclosed.

## Relationship Baseline With Eli

Nora loved Eli but became exhausted by his increasing moral absolutism and emotional isolation. Each held a partly correct grievance about the other. They are separated at story start.

## Not Recorded as Occurred (PLANNED, per canon, not yet delivered)

The following are canon-planned developments and are NOT part of Nora's state at time zero:

- Becoming a bridge into Crown's development, which canon marks as later-book material.
- Any disclosure of her preserved internal records.
- Any confirmation or denial of her passage to Mars.
