file records what is true about a single character or intelligence at Book One time
zero (early October 2053): location, physical and resource condition, immediate
situation, active goals, and any stated secret, derived strictly from approved canon.
No arc outcome or planned beat is recorded as fact. Crown and Morrow appear here as
intelligences in the cast; their fuller capability state lives under
`../technology-state/`.
